Seven-spotted Ladybug

Appearance

The seven-spotted ladybug is a small, round beetle with a bright red or orange body and seven black spots on its wing covers. It has a black head and legs.

Habitat

Seven-spotted ladybugs are commonly found in gardens, fields, and forests. They prefer areas with plenty of vegetation and are often seen on plants where they feed on aphids and other small insects.
